## @Query Auto-Refresh Mechanism

SwiftData's @Query property wrapper automatically observes the ModelContext:
1. When `modelContext.insert(newRecording)` is called
2. And `modelContext.save()` is invoked
3. @Query detects the change and refreshes the `recordings` array
4. SwiftUI updates the List automatically

No manual refresh needed.
